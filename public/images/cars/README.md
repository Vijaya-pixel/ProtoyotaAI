# Car Images Folder

## How to Add Your Own Car Photos

Place your car images in this folder with the following exact filenames:

### Required Image Files:
- `corolla.jpg` - Toyota Corolla
- `camry.jpg` - Toyota Camry
- `camry-hybrid.jpg` - Toyota Camry Hybrid
- `prius.jpg` - Toyota Prius
- `crown.jpg` - Toyota Crown
- `gr86.jpg` - Toyota GR86
- `supra.jpg` - Toyota GR Supra
- `rav4.jpg` - Toyota RAV4
- `rav4-hybrid.jpg` - Toyota RAV4 Hybrid
- `highlander.jpg` - Toyota Highlander
- `grand-highlander.jpg` - Toyota Grand Highlander
- `4runner.jpg` - Toyota 4Runner
- `venza.jpg` - Toyota Venza
- `sequoia.jpg` - Toyota Sequoia
- `sienna.jpg` - Toyota Sienna
- `tacoma.jpg` - Toyota Tacoma
- `tundra.jpg` - Toyota Tundra
- `bz4x.jpg` - Toyota bZ4X

### Supported Formats:
- `.jpg` or `.jpeg`
- `.png`
- `.webp`

### Recommendations:
- Image dimensions: 800x600 pixels or similar aspect ratio (4:3 or 16:9)
- File size: Keep under 500KB for faster loading
- Clear side or 3/4 view of the vehicle
- Good lighting and clean background

### Instructions:
1. Save your car photos to your computer
2. Rename them to match the exact filenames above
3. Copy/paste them into this folder: `ProtoyotaAI/public/images/cars/`
4. Refresh your browser to see your photos

**Note:** If an image is missing, it will show a broken image icon. Make sure all filenames match exactly (case-sensitive).
